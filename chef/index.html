GoCD AND CHEF
THE BEST RECIPE FOR CONTINUOUS DELIVERY

Continuous Delivery with GoCD on Chef

Step 1: Add the Cookbook

Add the cookbook to your Chef Server, either by installing with knife or by adding it to your Berksfile.

STEP 2: Configure Server and Agent

Change the default configuration or attributes of your GoCD server and agent.

STEP 3: Install GoCD and get started!

Use our recipes to install and run the GoCD server and agent. Once you have, GoCD is set up and ready to use.

Get started with continuous delivery on GoCD with your Chef configurations quickly with our official partner cookbook. This cookbook will help you setup GoCD servers and agents in a fully automated way. Choose your preferred method of installation, configure your server and agents to suit the needs of your team and run the recipes to install your GoCD server and agents.
